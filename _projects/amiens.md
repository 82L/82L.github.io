<p>This project is here, as it is my first 3D experience.</p>
<p>The teacher gave us the 3D file of the cathedral, and tasked us to add a few things:</p>
<ul>
<li>Fps Controls</li>
<li>Lights</li>
<li>Points of interests, where when you're into them, they open a popup</li>
</ul>
<p>All of these addons where to be made by us, without any help, or roadmaps. I'm quite proud of the implementation of the head movements with the camera. And of the movements of the character, which where made by using trigonometry</p>

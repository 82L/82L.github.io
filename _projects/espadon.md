<p>Animate is one of the worst software to develop a video game. I kind of gave up to make it perfect.</p>
<p>The goal was to make an interactive software with animate, with an interesting proposition in it</p>
<p>Here I decided to bring alive the Edgar P. Jacobs albums of Blake and Mortimer. By following the story of the firsts albums.</p>
<p>My ambition was a bit too big for the deadline given, so I narrowed down the scope, by transforming the shooter idea, to an endless shooter, with enemy waves, populated by one single type of plane.</p>
<p>There was a lot of material to exploit, and I'm kind of sad to haven't been able to use it.</p>
